About us

Kal_Edu continues a tradition of a downtown notestore that began in Mount Vernon in the twentieth century. In a time of big-box stores and e-readers, Kal_Edu is an independent, neighborhood Notesstore, a place with real books, real people, and real conversations, and a reminder that the turning of a page will always take us where we wish to go.
